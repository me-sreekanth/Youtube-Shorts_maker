Thomas Anderson.
Boring office guy.
Night hacker, Neo.
Feels like something's off.
Glitches, repeating patterns.
Messages: "Wake up."
Rumors of Morpheus.
Dangerous guy.
He finally meets Morpheus.
The world isn’t real.
His life’s a computer program.
The Matrix was built by machines.
Humans at war with machines.
They block sunlight, fail.
Machines turn humans into batteries.
Create the Matrix—a fake reality.
Morpheus offers a choice.
Blue pill—ignore the truth.
